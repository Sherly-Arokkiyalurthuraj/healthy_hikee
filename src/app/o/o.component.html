Daily Meal Plans

Breakfast

Oats idli with sambar (limit coconut chutney)

OR Vegetable upma with less oil

Mid-Morning Snack

1 fruit (e.g., apple) and green tea

Lunch

Brown rice or chapatis with dal, vegetable curry, salad

Avoid fried foods and minimize oil

Afternoon Snack

Sprouts salad or roasted chickpeas

Evening Snack

Clear vegetable soup

Dinner

Grilled fish with steamed vegetables

OR Mixed vegetable stir-fry with tofu

Nutrition Tips

  • Focus on low-calorie, nutrient-dense foods.
  • Include high-fiber foods to promote satiety.
  • Stay hydrated and avoid sugary drinks.
  • Practice portion control and avoid overeating.
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains.
  • Incorporate lean proteins and healthy fats.
