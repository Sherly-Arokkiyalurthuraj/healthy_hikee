Daily Meal Plans

Breakfast

Masala dosa with sambar and chutney

OR Vegetable upma with extra nuts and seeds

Mid-Morning Snack

Smoothie made with milk, banana, nuts, and seeds

Lunch

Brown rice or chapatis with dal, vegetable curry, salad

+ A serving of paneer or tofu

Afternoon Snack

Greek yogurt with honey and mixed nuts

Evening Snack

Vegetable soup with added beans or lentils

Dinner

Grilled chicken/fish with steamed vegetables and a side of quinoa

OR Paneer curry with chapatis and salad

Nutrition Tips

  • Include calorie-dense foods like nuts, seeds, and dairy.
  • Eat small, frequent meals throughout the day.
  • Stay hydrated with nutritious beverages like smoothies and milk.
  • Incorporate healthy fats in your diet.
  • Maintain a balance of macronutrients in each meal.
  • Ensure adequate protein intake to support muscle growth.
