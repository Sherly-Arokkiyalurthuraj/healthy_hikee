Daily Meal Plans

Breakfast

Oats idli with sambar (avoid chutney)

OR Vegetable upma with minimal oil

Mid-Morning Snack

1 fruit and green tea (unsweetened)

Lunch

Brown rice or chapatis with dal, non-starchy vegetable curry, salad

Limit portion sizes and avoid fried foods

Afternoon Snack

Sprouts salad or a handful of roasted chickpeas

Evening Snack

Clear vegetable soup

Dinner

Grilled fish/chicken with steamed vegetables

OR Mixed vegetable stir-fry with tofu

Nutrition Tips

  • Focus on low-calorie, high-fiber, and high-protein foods.
  • Include plenty of non-starchy vegetables in meals.
  • Drink water before meals to promote fullness.
  • Avoid sugary and processed foods.
  • Practice mindful eating and portion control.
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains.
  • Also go and consult the doctor.
